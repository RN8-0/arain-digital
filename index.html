About RN8

Building Digital
Products.

RN8 is an independent app studio. No team of 50, no VC money, no fluff — just focused product work. Every app is designed, built, and shipped end-to-end: product thinking, UI/UX, native development, backend, and deployment.

The first product was Sticky — a native Android app built in Kotlin that turns photos, videos, and GIFs into WhatsApp sticker packs. AI background removal, a full photo editor, and a cloud sticker library are all packed into a single app. Live on Google Play.

The second product is Qor AI — a cross-platform Flutter app that helps people make smarter decisions when buying tech products or choosing subscriptions. It combines AI-powered recommendations with smart side-by-side comparisons and subscription tracking. Backend runs on PocketBase. Live on Android, iOS coming soon.

Both products are live, maintained, and actively developed. More are in the works — always solving a real problem, never building for the sake of building.
